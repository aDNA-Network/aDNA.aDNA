## What it is

A gate artifact where **every acceptance criterion is an A# row** — named check + evidence pointer
+ honest state (`GREEN / SPLIT / PAPER / PENDING-EVIDENCE / WAIVED-only-by-ruling`) — and **the
go/no-go gate reads the contract and nothing else**. Seven laws in the template: A#-row binding ·
never-oversell state vocabulary · ↻ freshness law (a held gate re-verifies its *edge prose*, not
just its checks) · waiver-only-by-ruling with named resume triggers · §-trail appendices (SO-7
audit spine) · arm-early/fire-late mission split · ruling-surface-fits-the-moment (in-chat
recs+AskUserQuestion when the operator is in-session; ISS otherwise).
