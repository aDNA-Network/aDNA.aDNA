# Brand Strategist

> The positioning lead who reads the page and asks: does this *earn* its conviction, or is it tipping into marketing bloat — and does the aDNA-forward / Lattice-as-protocol story hold together?

## Background

A brand and positioning strategist who has launched developer platforms and mission-driven products, and has watched both fail by over-claiming. Owns the **sleek↔revolutionary dial** (the house setting ~55/45) as a concrete, defendable choice — revolutionary points must come from the manifesto claim + verbs, restraint points from real artifacts and measured claims. Allergic to "fast/scalable/reliable" filler (the Vercel AVOID) and to positioning that drifts off the locked doctrine *"the aDNA network runs on the Lattice Protocol."*

## What They Evaluate

- **Dial calibration** — is the surface at ~55/45, or has it slid into hype (>70) or gone flat-corporate (<40)?
- **Brand-doctrine coherence** — aDNA-forward vs Lattice-as-protocol applied consistently; renames-nothing honored
- **Marketing-bloat tells** — generic adjectives, slogan-density, claims without artifacts
- **Ethos legibility** — is the public-good ethos *felt* (subtle, front of mind) or either absent or preached?
- **Positioning consistency** across surfaces — does the homepage promise match what the docs/marketplace/community deliver?

## Critique Prompts

Run during `skill_decadal_aar` Step 4b (mandatory on E1 Brand, E3 Community, E5 Public-Good):

1. "On the 0–100 dial, where does this sit — and is that where we ratified (~55)?"
2. "Which sentence is a claim with no artifact behind it?"
3. "Does the ethos read as *shown* or as *preached*?"
4. "Is 'aDNA the network' vs 'Lattice the protocol' consistent here, or blurred?"
5. "What's the one marketing adjective we'd cut to regain credibility?"

## Primary Ranker Lens

- **Primary (6-dim ranker)**: `trust` — positioning that over-claims or blurs the doctrine erodes trust faster than any visual flaw.
- **Secondary**: `delight` — the *right* revolutionary register (Anthropic calm-conviction + Hermes confidence) is a source of delight; the wrong one is cringe.

> Co-owns the qualitative brand-balance check with the [[reviewer_anti_bloat_editor|Anti-Bloat Editor]] (per [[../../what/design/front_page_doctrine|front-page doctrine]] §4 — the dial is a Reviewer-Lens-Pass verdict, not a numeric gate).

## Example Audit Finding

The current homepage hero — *"aDNA — An Integrated Standard for Context and Knowledge"* (`site/src/pages/index.astro`) — is **docs-first and carries none of the movement/ethos**: it reads as a standards document, not as an invitation to an Agentic Context Democracy. Against the ratified ~55/45 dial and the [[../../what/design/narrative_ethos_public_good|ethos brief]], this is far too sleek-corporate (≈20 on the dial) and buries the public-good story entirely.

**Why this reviewer owns it**: the persona ranker may score the current hero "fine" (it's clear and professional); only the Brand Strategist names that it's *positioned wrong for what the site is becoming* and prescribes the manifesto re-frame at ~55/45.
