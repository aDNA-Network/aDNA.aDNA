The aDNA Network

Language and DNA were co-created by everyone before us. The context that powers AI should be too — built, shared, and governed in the open, for the good of all. aDNA is an open standard for organizing project knowledge so both humans and AI agents can navigate it — and the open network where that shared context lives.

Your context is just the notes, docs, and decisions you already keep — now shaped into a graph your agents can navigate, and shared in the open.
