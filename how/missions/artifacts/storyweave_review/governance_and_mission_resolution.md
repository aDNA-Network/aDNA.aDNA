# Governance + mission resolution — progressive decentralization

> **Why this exists.** The O4 red-team's single sharpest finding (both skeptics, independently) was that the site's **"context democracy"** claim contradicts its **"Founding Architect stewardship"** governance — *declared, not earned* (`o4_redteam_matrix.md` §B gap #2; T2). The operator (the Founding Architect) resolved it on **2026-07-07**. Captured here faithfully as input to O6/O7 and to O4 **priority item #2** (the credibility surface). **Planning-only — the site + canonical governance update at O8/build.**

## 1. The doctrine (faithful)

**The aDNA Network commits to progressive decentralization.**
- The network is **stewarded by a Founding Architect (FA) today**.
- As it **finds and onboards trusted stewards**, those stewards enter the governance process **increasingly** — as far as is **helpful and positive** — **at the FA's discretion**.
- That **discretion is eventually turned over to stewardship** itself.
- **Eventual goal:** the entire protocol/network is **steward-led · democratic · public**.

**Steward recruitment is mission-aligned.** Stewards are sought among the people closest to the network's **core mission areas**:
1. **Rare diseases**
2. **People living with undiagnosed disease**
3. **Biodiversity protection** *(NEW — confirmed absent from the site today; thematically coherent with the genome/DNA framing via conservation genomics)*

## 2. How this resolves the skeptic gap (T2)

The contradiction wasn't the governance model — it was **stating "democracy" as a present fact** while the docs described a single Founding Architect. Progressive decentralization converts that into an **honest roadmap**: FA *today* → increasing steward involvement → steward-led/public *destination*. This is precisely the "governance roadmap from Founding-Architect → multi-stakeholder" **both skeptics named as their single most-converting move.** The democratic aspiration is **kept** — but now it reads as an *earned destination*, not an unearned claim.
> **Nuance for O6/O7:** the fix is **additive** (state current state + roadmap), not deletion of the manifesto's "democracy." Reconcile the home manifesto's "self-governed context democracy" with one honest current-state line + the roadmap so aspiration and truth sit together. The register guardrail holds (calm/honest, never hype) — this *is* the honesty the skeptics said is the site's best asset.
