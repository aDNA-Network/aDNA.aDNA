# site_ethereum — ethereum.org

> **The closest conceptual twin** to "Agentic Context Democracy": a thing that must be a **movement** ("the internet that belongs to you") *and* a credible **protocol** (specs, EIPs, uptime) at the same time. The single best study for how aDNA holds movement-energy + protocol-credibility together.

## Captured (rubric)

- **Hero:** ~30 words. "The internet that belongs to you" — ownership/decentralization framing vs centralized platforms.
- **Sections (7):** hero → "Built to last" (10 years, 100% uptime) → "What makes Ethereum different" (4 principle cards) → "Get started" (3 pathways: Learn/Build/Enterprise) → use enumeration (DeFi/NFTs/DAOs/stablecoins) → community/events → footer (6 columns).
- **Above-fold focus:** hero **then immediately** the credibility stat — emotional lede validated by engineering track record.
- **Movement signals:** "belongs to you," "no company runs it," democratic governance, "no credit check, no paperwork, no minimum balance."
- **Protocol signals:** "10 years / 100% uptime," whitepaper, EIPs, developer tools, bold stats (311M holders, $4.6B daily volume).
- **Visual:** medium-sparse, generous whitespace, short copy (2–3 sentences/section); illustration-heavy (futuristic city/community); light bg + accent.
- **Nav:** breadth-first top menu (15+ categories) + 3 role-based card pathways (self-identify as learner/dev/business). Hub-and-spoke.

## Lift for aDNA

- **Lead emotionally, validate immediately with credibility** — the manifesto hero ("Agentic Context Democracy") followed by a concrete network/standard credibility anchor (MIT, open spec, real vaults). This is the exact sequence the doctrine recommends.
- **Role-based "get started" pathways** (self-ID) — mirror for aDNA's actor personas (build a vault / publish a graph / run a node / join the community).
- **Low-barrier framing** ("no paperwork...") translates to aDNA's "local-first, open, free" participation scent.

## Avoid

- 7 sections + 15+ nav categories edges toward sprawl; aDNA homepage holds **5 ±1** (the role pathways collapse into one "join the network" section).
