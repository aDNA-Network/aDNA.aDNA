# site_anthropic — Anthropic

> **The tone calibration** for "good of humanity" — how to carry a serious mission register *without zealotry or hype*. The single best reference for keeping aDNA's "for the good of humanity / abundance" framing credible rather than grandiose.

## Captured (rubric)

- **Hero:** ~25 words. "AI research and products that put safety at the frontier" + "securing its benefits and mitigating its risks." Deliberately understated; safety as *organizing principle*, not defensive constraint.
- **Sections (5):** hero → a concrete initiative → latest releases (3) → philosophy statement ("serve humanity's long-term well-being") → 5 linked commitments. Balances abstract mission with *tangible work*.
- **Above-fold focus:** the mission statement (words).
- **Tone register — the lesson:** **specificity over sentiment.** "Claude helped NASA's Perseverance rover travel four hundred meters on Mars" (measurable). "No ads. No sponsored content." (operational choice, not slogan). Minimal adjectives; **avoids "revolutionary / transformative / visionary."** Links to *actual* policy documents (Responsible Scaling Policy, Constitution) = substance. No "!", no "we believe."
- **Visual:** monochromatic hierarchy, generous negative space, no gradients/motion, loose type density — *restraint signals confidence*.
- **Nav:** dual-layer; mission/commitments live in primary nav (equal to products), not buried.

## Lift for aDNA

- **Sound serious because you *act* serious** — back the "context democracy" claim with concrete artifacts (a real governed vault, the open spec, a real federation) and *linked governance* (ADRs, the standard), not aspiration language. This is the antidote to the "revolutionary → cringe" failure mode.
- **Put the mission in the nav as a peer**, not a buried "about."
- **Restraint = confidence** reinforces the doctrine's ~55/45 dial: revolutionary in *substance/claim*, restrained in *register*.

## Avoid

- Anthropic's near-zero-energy calm would underplay aDNA's genuinely exciting/novel angle. aDNA wants Anthropic's *register* but a touch more Hermes-energy (hence ~55/45, not 40/60).
