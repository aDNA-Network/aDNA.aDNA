# site_replicate — Replicate

> **The live-affordance registry exemplar** — every registry entry is *executable, proven, and social*. Directly informs aDNA's marketplace/registry entry treatment (Track B) and the `/vaults` cards (Track D).

## Captured (rubric)

- **Hero:** ~25 words. "Run and fine-tune models. Deploy custom models. All with one line of code." / "Run AI with an API." Frames *access* as the value, not catalog browsing.
- **Registry vs marketing:** **marketing-first** — leads with a generated-output hero (visual appeal from featured models), registry appears downstream. Positions Replicate as enabling creators, not as a catalog.
- **Entry presentation (the lesson):** each model entry = **"Try" link** + **run count** ("29.4M runs") + **one-line code snippet** + "Official" badge + creator avatar. Entries are *executable* (one line), *proven* (counts), *social* (attribution) — not passive listings.
- **Sections (8):** hero+code → featured carousel → capability categories → creator grid → "how it works" (run/fine-tune/deploy) → enterprise → logos → "imagine what you can build."
- **Density:** high; carousels + grids; repetition signals abundance.
- **Visual:** clean sans-serif, generous whitespace; generated imagery dominates; card-based; avatar-heavy (community identity).

## Lift for aDNA

- **Every registry entry needs a live affordance.** For aDNA: each vault/forge/context-graph card should offer a concrete next action — "pull," "compose," "view graph," "open docs" — plus a proof signal (status/phase, federation count, last-updated) and creator/persona attribution. This is the marketplace-card spec seed.
- **Creator avatars + attribution** make a registry feel like a *community of builders*, not a database.

## Avoid

- **Marketing-first generated-output hero** suits an image-gen product; aDNA's hero should be the *manifesto* (Hermes) with the registry as section 2 — our artifacts are graphs/vaults, not eye-candy renders.
- **Repetition-as-abundance** (60+ entries repeated) reads as padding at small scale; curate (Val Town pattern) instead.
